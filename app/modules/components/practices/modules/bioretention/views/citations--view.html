ADDITIONAL METRIC INFORMATION

Impervious area draining to BMP (sf): The impervious area contributing runoff to the bioretention area with units in square feet.

Area draining to the BMP (DA, sf): The total drainage area contributing runoff to the bioretention area with units in square feet.

Runoff volume captured (EP, acre-ft): This is the total runoff volume stored in the bioretention area as calculated by the state-specific Engineering Parameter (EP) in acre-feet (CSN, 2015).

Rainfall depth Treated (RT, inches This is the depth of rainfall treated by the BMP in inches, as calculated by the formula RT = EP*12/(IA/43560), as described in CSN (2015).

USWG Adjustor Curve Efficiencies (%): These are the CBP USWG Runoff Reduction (RR) efficients for new retrofits, acalutated as a function of RT (CSN, 2013b).

Area of the BMP (sf): This is the area of the BMP itself, typically measured as length times width in square feet.

No. of observed bird or small animal species: As your bioretention area matures, count the number of bird and other animal species that begin to populate the area Sellner et al., 2012, pp.160-161.

Adaptive management checks: Summary Field Sheet (above); CSN, 2013a; CSN, 2014; Sellner et al., 2012.
