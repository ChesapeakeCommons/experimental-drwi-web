Table A. Evaluation of Recession Severity Category

Recession Category Lateral Recession Rate (ft/yr) Streambank and Channel Condition Description
Slight 0.03 Some bare bank but active erosion not readily apparent. Some rills but no vegetative overhang. No exposed tree roots.
Moderate 0.13 Bank is predominantly bare with some rills and vegetative overhang. Some exposed tree roots but no slumps or slips.
Severe 0.4 Bank is bare with rills and severe vegetative overhang. Many fallen trees, drains, and culverts eroding out and changes in cultural features such as fence corners missing and realignment of roads or trails. Channel cross-section becomes U-shaped as opposed to V-shaped.
Very Severe 0.6 Bank is bare with gullies and severe vegetative overhang. Many fallen trees, drains, and culverts eroding out and changes in cultural features such as fence corners missing and realignment of roads or trails. Massive slips or washouts common. Channel cross-section is U-shaped and stream course may be meandering.
Source: Wisconsin NRCS Field Office Technical Guide. 2003. Streambank erosion. Available at: http://dnr.wi.gov/runoff/pdf/grants/mso341_streambank.pdf
