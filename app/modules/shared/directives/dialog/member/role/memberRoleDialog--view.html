Members can view an organization’s data and settings. They can also view and edit its projects but are not allowed to create or delete them.

Members can view a project’s data and settings, including other members, sites, practices, and reports. They can edit sites, practices, and reports. Members are not allowed to delete any project-related features that they didn’t create themselves.

Owners have unrestricted access to an organization’s data and settings. They can add, modify, and delete projects and members.

Owners have unrestricted access to a project’s data and settings. They can add, modify, and delete any type of project-related feature, including members, sites, practices, and reports.
